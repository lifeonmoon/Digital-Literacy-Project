# Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1. [ ] **Never Share Your UPI PIN:** Remember that a UPI PIN is *only* required to send money or check your balance, never to receive money.
2. [ ] **Verify Payment Links:** Always double-check the recipient's name and the exact amount on your UPI app screen before authorizing any transaction.
3. [ ] **Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on all important accounts, including email, banking, and social media.
4. [ ] **Beware of Unsolicited Calls/Messages:** Do not trust callers claiming to be from your bank asking for OTPs or account details; legitimate banks never ask for this information over the phone.
5. [ ] **Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Transactions:** Never conduct financial transactions or log into sensitive accounts while connected to unsecured public Wi-Fi networks in cafes or airports.
6. [ ] **Keep Software Updated:** Regularly update your phone's OS, banking apps, and antivirus software to patch known security vulnerabilities.
7. [ ] **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Use a password manager to generate and store complex passwords instead of reusing the same password everywhere.
8. [ ] **Report Suspicious Activity Promptly:** If you detect unauthorized transactions or fall victim to fraud, immediately contact your bank to freeze your account.
   - **Report to:** National Cyber Crime Portal at [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
   - **Dial Helpline:** 1930 (24x7 National Cybercrime Reporting Helpline)
