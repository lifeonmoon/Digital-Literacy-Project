The  Scenario    You  have  just  been  selected  as  a  Student  Digital  Ambassador  at  your  college.  Your  role  is  to  help  your  peers  navigate  the  digital  world  —  from  creating  professional  online  profiles,  to  communicating  safely  and  responsibly  online.  Your  first  assignment  is  to  build  a  Digital  Literacy  Portfolio  that  you  will  present  to  the  faculty  committee  and  share  with  your  batch.

Name  the  repository:  digital-literacy-project.  Set  it  to  Public.  Check  'Add  a  README  
file'.
